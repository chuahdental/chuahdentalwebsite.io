About Dr. Chuah Kee Seng

Dr. Chuah pursued his higher education and obtained his Bachelor of Dental Surgery from the University of Malaya in 2005. He continued his studies there, earning a postgraduate degree in 2008.

Dr. Chuah subsequently obtained a postgraduate diploma in Advanced Aesthetic & Restorative Dentistry in London, UK. He is a diplomate of the AARD (UK). In addition to his studies, he served as a dental officer with attachments in the Oral-Surgery, Oral Medicine & Periodontology, Orthodontics, and Restorative Departments at the Faculty of Dentistry, University of Malaya, from 2005 to 2008. Dr. Chuah aims to provide painless dentistry at the most reasonable fees. He is particularly passionate about treating children and the elderly and works in all aspects of dental care.
